About the Studio

We don't design buildings.
We craft experiences.

Founded in 2009 by Elise Moreau and Jean-Paul Fontaine, FORMA has become one of Europe's most celebrated architecture and interior design studios. Our multidisciplinary team of 24 architects, designers, and craftspeople work at the intersection of art, culture, and built space.

Every project begins with a single question: what emotion should this space evoke? From that kernel, we develop spatial narratives that are both rigorously functional and deeply poetic.

How We Work

A methodology rooted in curiosity

Phase 01
Discovery & Immersion

We begin by listening. Every project starts with deep research into client vision, site history, cultural context, and material possibilities. We inhabit the problem before proposing any solution.

Client Workshops Site Analysis Precedent Research
Phase 02
Concept & Strategy

A single, powerful concept drives every decision. This conceptual anchor — spatial, material, or narrative — becomes the DNA from which all design choices evolve with coherence.

Mood Boards Spatial Narrative 3D Sketching
Phase 03
Design Development

Concept crystallizes into detailed drawings, material specifications, and technical documentation. We collaborate with structural engineers, craftspeople, and specialist consultants throughout.

Technical Drawings Material Palette Visualization
Phase 04
Craft & Realization

We remain intimately involved through construction and installation. Our on-site presence ensures that the built reality matches — and often surpasses — the designed intention.

Site Supervision Quality Control Artisan Collaboration
